ICRAT Sample Pages

The landing page has three different versions:

  • "Call for Papers" version, when the conference was announced and the templates are online
  • "Registration" version, when details for the conference (schedule, venues, hotels) are available
  • "Proceedings and upcoming conference" version, when the current conference is over, proceedings are online and the call for papers is not yet announced

In addition, the following separate pages are included:

  • List of proceedings and presentations
  • Sponsoring Opportunities
  • Conference Area Information
  • Hotel Information
  • Conference gallery
  • Registration form
